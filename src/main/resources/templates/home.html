About VaxConnect Sri Lanka

VaxConnect Sri Lanka is a comprehensive vaccination management platform developed in partnership with the Ministry of Health. Our mission is to make vaccination accessible, trackable, and secure for all Sri Lankan citizens.

We believe that every individual deserves easy access to vaccination services and the ability to maintain their health records in a secure, digital format. Our platform connects patients with healthcare providers, streamlines the vaccination process, and ensures that everyone stays protected against preventable diseases.
